# TABLEAU SALES & CUSTOMER INSIGHT VISUALIZATION PROJECT

    This project contains two interactive Tableau dashboards designed to help stakeholders—including sales managers, analysts, executives, and marketing teams—gain deeper insights into sales performance and customer behavior. The dashboards visualize year-over-year trends, key performance indicators (KPIs), and detailed comparisons to support data-driven decision-making.

## Sales Dashboard
    To provide an overview of key sales metrics and trend patterns, enabling stakeholders to evaluate year-over-year (YoY) performance, monitor monthly and weekly trends, and analyze product-level performance.

### 1. KPI Overview
      Displays total sales, profit, and quantity for:
          - Current Year
          - Previous Year

### 2. Sales Trends
      - Monthly trend charts for each KPI (Sales, Profit, Quantity) which includes the comparison between current year vs. previous year.
      - Highlights months with highest and lowest sales for quick recognition.

### 3. Product Subcategory Comparison
      - Compares sales performance across product subcategories.
      - Includes sales vs. profit comparison for deeper profitability analysis.

### 4. Weekly Trends for Sales & Profit
      - Weekly-level sales and profit visualizations for the current year.
      - Displays average weekly values.
      - Highlights weeks above and below the average to indicate performance deviations.

## Customer Dashboard
    To provide insights into customer demographics, purchasing behavior, order patterns, and profitability—supporting marketing strategies, customer segmentation, and engagement analysis.

### 1. KPI Overview
        Comparison shown for current year vs. previous year
          - Total number of customers
          - Total sales per customer
          - Total number of orders

### 2. Customer Trends
        - Monthly trends for customer-related KPIs.
        - Highlights months with the highest and lowest customer sales.

### 3. Customer Distribution by Number of Orders
        - Visual representation of customers grouped by their order frequency.
        - Helps understand loyalty, engagement, and purchase behavior.

### 4. Top 10 Customers by Profit
        Ranks the top 10 customers generating the highest profit. It displays:
            - Rank
            - Number of orders
            - Current year sales
            - Current year profit
            - Last order date

## Tools & Technologies
      - Tableau Desktop
      - Data Source: Sales & Customer dataset (CSV/Excel/Database)
      - Calculated fields, LOD expressions, parameters, YoY comparisons, and trend analytics
